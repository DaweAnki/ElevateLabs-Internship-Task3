## 💡 Key Business Insights  
- Technology category generates the highest sales  
- A small group of customers contributes significantly to total revenue  
- West region performs strongest in both sales and profit  
- Sales show a steady increase over the years  
- Certain sub-categories drive most of the profit  
- High-value orders can be identified using average sales benchmarks
